23 Feb. French, Paris

My first stop is French. Walking along the Seine river, I feel incredibly relaxed and free. I stopped for a rest with my friends at a cute-decorated dessert restaurant. Chocolate brownie tastes perfect with lucious bananas.

23 March. Poland, Warsaw

Warsaw is the sprawling capital of Poland. Its widely varied architecture reflects the city's long, turbulent history. This picture was shot in a small park near the old town. Wind breezed and water flowed. I felt like I was in a fairy tale.

23 April. Netherland Amsterdam

I was deeply impressed by its colorful street view and the beautiful canals of Amsterdam when stepping in this land. Just like this portait, which is a piece of unique work I've found in a small restaurant.

23 May. Stockholm Sweden

Stockholm is called the "Venice of the North". There's a beautiful church with oiled-green roof located in the city center. I took off this picture in a shiny day.

23 June. Barcelona Spain

You couldn't miss the iconic building: Sagrada Família, where Gaudí's visionary genius comes to life in a symphony of sculpted stone. I visited the church in the afternoon where the sunset light broke through the glass and created a fabulous painting work on the ground.
